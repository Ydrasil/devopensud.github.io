# DevOpenSud 2015 : Ephemeral coworkingin the middle-earth ## Digest This ephemeral coworking will be made by those who will be there * from Thursday, april 23th to Sunday, april 26th * An house in Auvergne (somewhere in the middle of the France) * No program. * No organiser. * 150 euros for food and bed for the week. * [Inscription](https://www.weezevent.com/dev-open-sud-2015). ## FAQ *The event will be made by those who will be there* #### What is DevOpenSud? Somewhere around sleeping volcanos, some developpers gather in a cottage to talk, code, walk, pair, collaborate, play, do things... #### Who organise that thing? There are ``s/not really/really no/`` organisers. #### How much does it costs? Price is set to 150 euros for the whole week, it should be exactly what will be spend in hosting and food. #### What is included for that price? From Thursday, april 23th in the morning to sunday, april 26th: all your meal and bed. #### One full week? Yes. One full week! But you can stay only for one day, or more. It's up to you. #### Can I come to work during the day and play with you at night? Yes, Dev Open Sud 2015 is also an ephemeral co-working space. #### Should I pay something now? No, we trust you. You will pay everything on site. But please, alert us if you defect. #### Can I have an invoice? Yes, we can do that. #### Where do I sign? [https://www.weezevent.com/dev-open-sud-2015](https://www.weezevent.com/dev-open-sud-2015). #### How many people will be there? Maybe 8, maximum 15, probably around 10. #### Is there some carpooling planned to go to the event? Well, ``we`` won't planned anything, but ``you`` can use the [e-mailing list](mailto:devopensud-2015@googlegroups.com) to organise it :) #### Can I have a room with no snorer? You probably could find a way to organize a room with no snorer. But, maybe it could be a good idea to come with earplugs :) #### What will we eat? It is not decide yet. But if you have some food constraints (no gluten, allergies, vegan, ...) please, tell us as soon as possible so we can take care of it. #### By the way, where is it? > [https://www.airbnb.fr/rooms/4136472](https://www.airbnb.fr/rooms/4136472) #### What if I have a question? You can contact Antoine - [@avernois](http://twitter.com/avernois) - or Stéphane - [@langlois_s](http://twitter.com/langlois_s) You can also use the IRC channel ##devopensud on freenode, or subscribe to the mailing-list: [devopensud-2015@googlegroups.com](mailto:devopensud-2015@googlegroups.com) ## But your english is awfull? Yes, we know. We are french.
