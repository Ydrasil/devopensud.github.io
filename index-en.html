# Digest The event will be made by those who will be there * from monday, avril 20th to sunday, avril 26th * A big house in Auvergne (somewhere in the middle of the France) * No program. * No organiser. * 250 euros for food and bed for the week or 50 euros per day. * [Inscription](https://www.weezevent.com/dev-open-sud-2015). # FAQ *The event will be made by those who will be there* #### What is DevOpenSud? Somewhere around sleeping volcanos, some developpers gather in a cottage to talk, code, walk, pair, collaborate, play, do things... #### Who organise that thing? There are ``s/not really/really no/`` organisers. #### How much does it costs? Price is set to 250 euros for the whole week or 50 euros per day, it should be exactly what will be spend in hosting and food. #### What is included for that price? From monday, april 20th in the morning to sunday, april 26th: all your meal and bed. #### One full week? Yes. One full week! But you can stay only for one day, or more. It's up to you. #### Can I come to work during the day and play with you at night? Yes, Dev Open Sud 2015 is also an ephemeral co-working space. #### Should I pay something now? No, we trust you. You will pay everything on site. But please, alert us if you defect. #### Can I have an invoice? Yes, we can do that. #### Where do I sign? [https://www.weezevent.com/dev-open-sud-2015](https://www.weezevent.com/dev-open-sud-2015). #### How many people will be there? Maybe 10, maximum 25, probably around 15. #### Is there some carpooling planned to go to the event? Well, ``we`` won't planned anything, but ``you`` can use the [e-mailing list](mailto:devopensud-2015@googlegroups.com) to organise it :) #### Can I have a room with no snorer? You probably could find a way to organize a room with no snorer. But, maybe it could be a good idea to come with earplugs :) #### What will we eat? It is not decide yet. But if you have some food constraints (no gluten, allergies, vegan, ...) please, tell us as soon as possible so we can take care of it. #### By the way, where is it? In Saviloisirs, place de la liberté, Saint Amand-Roche-Savine, France. #### Is there a website or pictures so I can dream of it?
